About UR

UR is an advanced cryptocurrency made for everyday people and everyday expenditures. 2,000 UR is distributed to every human being who chooses to join the UR community and proves his/her identity. There is no cost to join or to receive this distribution. UR community members may earn additional UR by referring and for helping to support the integrity, security, and usefulness of UR currency and the UR community. (learn more about UR)

UR Capital is a corporation whose mission is to create a secure, stable, and useful world currency available to every human being.
